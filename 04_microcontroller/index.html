Given that my electronics were such a staggering fail, I decided to practice a core skill that would be useful in future projects -- soldering. Conclusion

This week was rough. Microcontrollers are so fickle -- it's unclear what could be going wrong, and even more frustrating, it's totally unclear how to diagnose what might be going wrong -- especially when the issues may be physical, or may be a busted board, or a loose wire. Sigh.
